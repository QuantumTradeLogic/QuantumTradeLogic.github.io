About the Firm

A private holding company built for permanence

Perma Holdings LLC is a private family holding company founded in 2022 with a singular mandate: preserve and grow capital across generations through disciplined market participation and selective private investments.

The firm's name reflects its founding philosophy — permanence as a competitive advantage. Where most capital chases quarterly returns, we operate on a time horizon that allows us to hold positions others cannot.
