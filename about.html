About Perma Holdings

A private holding company built on the conviction that permanence is the ultimate competitive advantage in capital markets.

Perma Holdings LLC was founded in July 2022 with a clear and enduring mandate: preserve and grow family capital through disciplined participation in public markets and selective investments in private ventures. The firm operates as a private holding company — patient, focused, and built for the long term.

The name “Perma” is not incidental. It reflects the firm’s foundational belief that permanence — in capital, in conviction, in relationships — is a structural advantage that most institutional participants cannot replicate. Quarterly earnings pressure, fund lifecycles, and investor redemptions force the majority of capital into short-term behavior. Perma Holdings is structured to be free of those constraints.

The firm’s activities span two complementary domains. In public equity markets, the principals employ quantitatively informed trading strategies that prioritize risk discipline and capital preservation over speculative gain. In private markets, the firm holds selective positions in early-stage ventures where deep conviction exists in the founding team, the market structure, and the long-term durability of the opportunity.

Perma Holdings does not manage outside capital, does not accept investor subscriptions, and does not publish performance data. It is a private family holding entity, accountable only to the long-term financial health and integrity of the capital it stewards.

What We Believe

Philosophy

Every decision at Perma Holdings flows from a small number of durable principles: capital preservation takes precedence over growth; time horizon is a weapon when others don’t have it; and conviction, not momentum, should determine position sizing.

These are not novel ideas — they are the operating principles of the most successful long-duration capital allocators in history. Our work is simply to apply them with consistency and discipline, year after year, in both public and private markets.
